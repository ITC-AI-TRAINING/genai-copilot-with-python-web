About JokeApp

Project Overview

JokeApp is a modern, interactive web application built with Flask that brings laughter and entertainment to your fingertips. Our application leverages the powerful JokeAPI to deliver a diverse collection of jokes across multiple categories including Programming, Miscellaneous, and Dark humor. Whether you're looking for a quick laugh during a coding session or need a mood booster, JokeApp has you covered!
